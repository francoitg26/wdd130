About Us

Welcome to White Water Rafting Co.! Our mission is to bring people together through thrilling river adventures, professional guides, and unforgettable outdoor experiences. We are committed to safety, sustainability, and fun!

Our History

Since 1995, White Water Rafting Co. has taken thousands of adventurers down some of the most scenic and exhilarating rivers in the country. What started as a small family business has grown into a leading outdoor company built on trust, adventure, and unforgettable memories.
