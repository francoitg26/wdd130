About Us

Our History

White Water Rafting Co. was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate explorers who wanted to share their love of rivers with the world. Over the decades, we've grown into a leading adventure company known for safety, fun, and unforgettable experiences.

Our journey began on the Colorado River and has since expanded to multiple rivers across the country. We've guided over 50,000 happy rafters and counting!

Adventure Awaits You!

From family-friendly floats to intense whitewater challenges, our adventures are designed for every thrill-seeker. Our trained guides ensure your safety while making your trip exciting and memorable.
